Introduction

Dawood University of Engineering & Technology has introduced a system that solved the measure problem regarding the points(buses) system in Dawood university. the new smart system will help both the students and transport management to monitor the location of every point(buses) running in their respective root. So it will help to students pick their respective point(buses) on time.
